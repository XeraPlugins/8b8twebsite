Overview

Welcome to 8b8t, 8b8t is one of the most popular anarchy servers and has been running under the names 0b0t, 6b6t, 8b8t for over 10 years. As a popular 'alternative' to 2b2t we offer a very different experience of Minecraft anarchy without massive wait times. Here on 8b8t, everything in-game is allowed swearing, griefing, hacking, etc.

Our core philosphy at 8b8t is that we want to be a player driven server. Every new change added to the server will center around the current playerbase and we constantly improve our server. Being one of the oldest anarchy servers we have seen the rise and fall of many anarchy servers many of those who no longer stick to the core values of anarchy. Examples include enforcing rules that ruin the experience and makes it seems like an SMP or botting servers / artificially inflating player counts to make it seem like your server is more popular. 8b8t has restrained itself from doing it we realized that the players are what matter not the number. Being one of the last basitions of Minecraft anarchy has gave us a clear path forward of what we should do we shouldn't try and copy other servers and we should make our own atmosphere.

Regardless, 8b8t continues to thrive and has many groups on the server our spawn is one of the most destroyed and has undergone many transformations over the years making it quite difficult to escape. Join our community, it's free.

Where it started

8b8t originally started in late 2016 under the name 0b0t as a non-vanilla anarchy server created by Leee as an alternative to the server 2b2t due to the long queue times. The server was then rebranded to 6b6t sometime in 2019 due to losing the domain.

6b6t's current map began on March 3, 2019 and stayed online until April 2020 when there was a hardware issue and this former map was thought to be lost. A new map began sometime in April 2020 and the server became what is now known as 8b8t.

The current world is 6 years old and was created on March 3, 2019.

Technologies & Mechanics

8b8t runs on a highly customized Folia fork, allowing for multi-threaded performance that traditional servers cannot match. Our core plugin, 8b8tCore, is the most advanced anti-illegal system ever made, protecting the server at the bitstream level.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is hacking allowed on 8b8t.me?

Yes. 8b8t is an anarchy server with no in-game rules.

Why is 8b8t a cracked server?

8b8t is a cracked server because of Mojangs EULA. 8b8t goes against Mojang pollitics, we believe in player freedom and choice.

Why do illegal events exist?

Illegal events are a way to make money from the server. We do not profit off our server and our anti-illegal system allows us to revert items after the illegal event. You can still keep your illegals but you cannot use them after events illegals massively accelerate destruction or creation.

Why is there /homes, /tpa, etc.?

Being the very first non-vanilla anarchy server, we had streamlined most of the tedious tasks our server is not like 9b9t/Constantiam/2b2t while many players like the 2b2t format the majority of our playerbase prefers such commands, dupes, etc as it makes it easier to make massive builds or do big projects with less players.

What is the seed of 8b8t?

The seed is hashed with the Blake3 Digest algorithim this means that it's a one way function to protect against world terrain exploitation such as block rotations, bedrock, and terrain we keep the salt private. The old seed is now obselete however for old terrain generation the seed is -9079062558503125353
