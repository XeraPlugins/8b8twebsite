Overview

8b8t stands as one of the most enduring and authentic anarchy servers in Minecraft history. Evolving through names like 0b0t, 6b6t, and now 8b8t, this project has delivered pure, unrestricted chaos for over a decade. As a leading alternative to 2b2t, we offer the raw anarchy experience you crave—without the endless queue times. Here, absolutely everything is permitted: swearing, griefing, hacking, duping, PvP, and beyond. No rules. No staff interference. No resets.

Our core philosophy is simple: the server is player-driven. Every update, feature, and decision revolves around what the current community wants and needs. We've continuously refined performance, stability, and gameplay to keep the experience fresh and true to anarchy's roots. Unlike many "anarchy" servers that have drifted from the original ideals—by adding rules, turning into glorified SMPs, botting fake player counts, or chasing artificial popularity—8b8t has stayed committed. We prioritize real players over inflated numbers. As one of the last true bastions of unrestricted Minecraft, we've carved our own path: no copying others, just building a unique, chaotic atmosphere shaped by the community.

Our spawn remains one of the most brutally destroyed and transformed battlegrounds in anarchy history—making every escape a true test of skill and determination. Active groups, alliances, and rivalries keep the server alive and unpredictable. Join the chaos today—it's completely free. Come build (or destroy), survive, and become part of 8b8t's legacy.

Where it started

8b8t originally started in late 2016 under the name 0b0t as a non-vanilla anarchy server created by Leee as an alternative to the server 2b2t due to the long queue times. The server was then rebranded to 6b6t sometime in 2019 due to losing the domain.

6b6t's current map began on March 3, 2019 and stayed online until April 2020 when there was a hardware issue and this former map was thought to be lost. A new map began sometime in January 2021 and the server became what is now known as 8b8t.

The current world is 6 years old and was created on August 18, 2020.

Technologies & Mechanics

8b8t runs on a highly customized Folia fork, allowing for multi-threaded performance that traditional servers cannot match. Our core plugin, 8b8tCore, is the most advanced anti-illegal system ever made, protecting the server at the bitstream level.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is hacking allowed on 8b8t.me?

Yes. 8b8t is an anarchy server with no in-game rules.

Why is 8b8t a cracked server?

8b8t is a cracked server because of Mojangs EULA. 8b8t goes against Mojang politics, we believe in player freedom, privacy and choice.

Why do illegal events exist?

Illegal events are a way to make money from the server. We do not profit off our server and our anti-illegal system allows us to revert items after the illegal event. You can still keep your illegals but you cannot use them after events illegals massively accelerate destruction or creation.

Why is there /homes, /tpa, etc.?

Being the very first non-vanilla anarchy server, we had streamlined most of the tedious tasks our server is not like 9b9t/Constantiam/2b2t while many players like the 2b2t format the majority of our playerbase prefers such commands, dupes, etc as it makes it easier to make massive builds or do big projects with less players.

What is the seed of 8b8t?

The seed is hashed with the Blake3 Digest algorithim this means that it's a one way function to protect against world terrain exploitation such as block rotations, bedrock, and terrain we keep the salt private.
