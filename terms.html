Overview

8b8t is a true anarchy Minecraft server. Players have complete freedom to act as they choose within the bounds of applicable law. There are no enforced rules against griefing, hacking, chat spam, toxicity, or other in-game behavior.

That said, illegal activity and content that causes real-world harm are strictly prohibited and will be addressed as required by law. This includes, but is not limited to, child sexual abuse material (CSAM), credible threats of violence, swatting, and any form of exploitation.

8b8t upholds a strong free speech policy for legal expression. The absence of gameplay rules does not exempt anyone from legal consequences for illegal conduct.

Compliance with Law

You must comply with all applicable laws, including those of Canada and your country of residence. Illegal activity conducted on or through our services will not be tolerated and may be reported to the relevant authorities.

Zero Tolerance for Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

8b8t has a zero-tolerance policy for Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and any content that depicts, promotes, or facilitates the sexual abuse or exploitation of minors. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Images, videos, text, descriptions, links, or references to child sexual exploitation in any form.
  • Illegal maparts, signs, books, chat messages, inventory or ender chest contents, or any other in-game medium containing or linking to such material.
  • Grooming, solicitation, or any preparatory conduct related to child exploitation.

Prohibited Actions

  • Uploading, storing, linking to, sharing, discussing in detail, or possessing CSAM in any form.
  • Sending links, private messages, or other content containing or referencing CSAM.
  • Attempting to report suspected CSAM to staff by sharing, screenshotting, describing in detail, or re-uploading the material.
  • Use of Kit Bots used to distribute kits on the server.

Reporting Suspected CSAM Do Not Contact Staff

If you encounter suspected CSAM, follow these steps:

  • Do not interact with, repeatedly view, save, screenshot, share, or forward the content.
  • Do not send it to 8b8t staff or post about it in any server, Discord, or Telegram channel. Doing so exposes staff to illegal material and creates legal liability.
  • Report it directly to the appropriate authorities immediately.

Canada: Canadian Centre for Child Protection — cybertip.ca

If you are outside Canada, contact your local law enforcement.

Staff Policy on CSAM

8b8t staff and administrators will not view, investigate, analyze, store, or handle suspected CSAM under any circumstances. Any attempt to send such material to staff — via reports, messages, links, or otherwise — will result in an immediate permanent ban and may be referred directly to law enforcement without review.

This policy exists to comply with our legal obligations, protect staff, and ensure that reports reach trained investigators and authorities who are equipped to handle them.
