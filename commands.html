Server Commands

Pretty much every command is listed here. If you can't find it, skill issue.

Command Description Rank Required
/tpa Teleports to another player Free
/tpahere Teleport a player to your location Free
/tpayes Accept a teleport request Free
/tpano Deny a teleport request Free
/tpacancel Cancel a pending teleport request Free
/back Return to your last location Free
/hotspot Teleport to a hotspot Free
/home Teleport to a set home Free
/delhome Delete a home you have saved Free
/sethome Set a home Free
/skin Changes your skin to any Minecraft user Free
/vote Link to the webpage to vote (legacy command) Free
/discord Get the discord info (legacy command) Free
/kill Kills your player Free
/ignore Ignores a player Free
/unignore Unignores a player Free
/ignorelist Shows your ignore list Free
/msg Message a player Free
/changepass Changes your password (Cracked players only) Free
/worldstats Displays world size and total players joined Free
/tpsinfo To display the TPS info in your region Free
/dps Toggle Phantom Spawning in the end Free
/achievements Toggles other player achievements in the chat Free
/deathmessage Toggles player death messages Free
/joindate Shows a players join date Free
/lastseen Shows when a player last joined Free
/toggleleaderboard Switches between the vanilla and custom 8b8t leaderboard Free
/togglejoinmessage Toggles player join messages on/off Free
/togglechat Turns chat on/off Free
/announcements Toggle announcements Voter/Rank
/cosmetics nick Changes your nickname Voter/Rank
/cosmetics item Changes item color Voter/Rank
/toggleprefix Toggles your [rank] tag Rank
/rename Renames an item Rank
/echest Opens your Ender Chest Rank
/hotspot create Creates a hotspot Ultra Rank
/cosmetics title Changes your title color Custom Rank
